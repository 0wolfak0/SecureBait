Metric Definitions

MetricDefinitionIdeal Trend
Email Open Rate Percentage of recipients who opened the simulation email. Tracked via embedded tracking pixel. 📉 Decrease over time (users learn to be cautious)
Click-Through Rate (CTR) Percentage of recipients who clicked the link in the simulation email. 📉 Decrease significantly over time
Credential Submission Rate Percentage of recipients who entered data on the training landing page. 📉 Should approach 0% with effective training
Reporting Rate Percentage of recipients who correctly reported the email as phishing. 📈 Increase over time (primary success indicator)
Time to Report Average time between email delivery and user reporting it as phishing. 📉 Decrease (faster detection = better awareness)
Training Completion Percentage of users who completed the post-click awareness training module. 📈 Should be 100% for clicked users

Awareness Improvement Metrics

Beyond individual campaign metrics, track these long-term improvement indicators:

📈

Susceptibility Reduction

Measure the percentage decrease in click-through rates between the first and most recent campaigns. Target: >50% reduction within 6 months.

⏱️

Response Time Improvement

Track how quickly users report suspicious emails. Faster reporting means better situational awareness and organizational resilience.

🎓

Training Engagement

Monitor completion rates of post-simulation training modules. High engagement correlates with sustained awareness improvement.

🏢

Departmental Analysis

Break down metrics by department to identify high-risk groups that need targeted training interventions.

Data Collection & Privacy

💡 Privacy Considerations

Even in simulations, follow data minimization principles. Collect only what's needed for training metrics. Anonymize individual results when reporting to management. The goal is to improve the organization's security posture, not to single out individuals.

  • Aggregate Reporting: Present results at team/department level, not individual level
  • Data Retention: Define a retention period for simulation data (e.g., 12 months)
  • Consent: Ensure participants are informed that security awareness testing is part of the training program
  • No Punitive Action: Simulation results should never be used for disciplinary purposes
